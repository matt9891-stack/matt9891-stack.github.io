## About

Hi, nice to meet you!

My name is Mattia, and I am a passionate Data Analyst from Italy.

My journey into technology began in 2020 when I first downloaded an Excel course to enhance my skills in data analysis and forecasting. As I explored Excel’s capabilities, I realised the need for more efficient ways to access and share data with my team.

This curiosity led me to study SQL and Google Looker, where I developed dashboards that highlighted key insights into departmental trends and top-selling products for the store I managed.

Over the next two years, my passion for technology continued to grow, prompting me to enrol in a Data Analytics Bootcamp. There, I expanded my skills in SQL, Python, and Tableau, equipping myself with the tools to turn raw data into actionable insights.

Driven by a desire to dive deeper into the world of Data Science, I embarked on a new chapter by enrolling in the MSc in Data Science at the University of Essex.

Now, with a solid foundation in data analytics and hands-on experience in retail business and management, I am excited to further develop my expertise and contribute to solving real-world problems through data.
